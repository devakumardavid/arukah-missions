The Arukah philosophy

Compassion should be intentional, humble, and measurable.

Arukah Missions exists to create more personal missions of support. Every individual and family can set aside a monthly or yearly pledge and commit to helping genuine needs in a systematic, organized way.

Our goal is not simply to raise funds for Arukah. Our goal is to inspire more people to create and manage their own missions of compassion.

How it works

From pledge to fulfilled mission

01

Take a Mission Pledge

Choose your mission category and monthly or yearly pledge commitment.

02

Receive Mission Identity

Your pledge is represented publicly by a private pseudo name, not your real name.

03

Support Verified Needs

Needs are reviewed and verified before support is recommended.

04

Fulfill with Dignity

Support is delivered responsibly through trusted providers wherever possible.

05

Measure Impact

Impact is tracked without exposing donor identity or recipient dignity.

Privacy by design

Let the mission be known. Let the giver remain humble.

Arukah Missions protects donor identity and recipient dignity. Public impact should inspire others, but it should never become a stage for recognition or exposure.

Private Donor Identity

Mission Partners are displayed using a Mission Identity instead of their real name.

Recipient Dignity

Recipient details are shared only when required for verification, fulfillment, and support.

Consent-Based Stories

Stories, images, and testimonials are shared only with consent and can be anonymized.
