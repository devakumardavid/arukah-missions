Meaning

Arukah means healing, restoration, and wholeness.

We exist to restore dignity through practical help. Instead of blind giving, we focus on verified needs, trusted service providers, and measurable outcomes.

Verify

Review documents, references, provider estimates, and local validation before approval.

Fulfill

Work directly with schools, hospitals, pharmacies, therapy centers, and vendors.

Prove

Close each case with receipts, photos where appropriate, provider confirmation, and outcome notes.

Workflow

How it works

1

Need Submitted

A beneficiary, volunteer, church, or partner submits a request.

2

Review

Team reviews need, urgency, documents, and provider details.

3

Verify

Local and provider validation confirms authenticity and amount.

4

Pay Provider

Payment is made directly to the service or product provider.

5

Close Mission

Receipt, proof, and impact summary are stored and shared.
